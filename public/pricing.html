FAQ

Do I need to pay to use Mongoose Studio?
No. The core Mongoose Studio functionality is free - no sign-up, no strings attached. Just npm install and go. The Solo and Pro plans give you access to optional add-ons that make it easy to manage access to your data.
Is there a trial period?
Yes, 14 day free trial on all plans.
Where is my data stored?
By default, dashboards, dashboard results, and chat history are stored in your MongoDB instance. Your data never leaves your cluster.

Not ready to buy?

You can use Mongoose Studio for free today. The quick start guide walks through getting it running in your app with no purchase required.
