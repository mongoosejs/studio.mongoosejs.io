## Linked Document References in JSON View

You can now directly navigate between related documents when viewing JSON data on your document pages! For any fields that reference another model, you'll see a "View Document" link next to the JSON value. Clicking it will instantly take you to that document's detail page for seamless browsing of linked data.

Other improvements:
- The JSON viewer is now more modular and easier to maintain for future enhancements.
- Collapsed and filter views continue to work as before, with reference links available wherever relevant.

This update makes it much easier to explore and understand relationships in your data!
