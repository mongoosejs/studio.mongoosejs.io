## Custom Date Picker

Mongoose Studio v0.5.8 replaces native date inputs with a custom date picker built for Studio's document and query workflows.
The picker includes calendar navigation, selectable days, and scrollable time controls for hours, minutes, and seconds.

<img src="https://res.cloudinary.com/drfhhq8wu/image/upload/v1781565735/Screenshot_from_2026-06-15_19-21-28_xsulje.png" alt="Custom date picker" />

## Date Filters

Model search now opens the date picker when your cursor is inside a `Date(...)` or `new Date(...)` helper.
Pick a date and time, choose whether to insert it as a millisecond timestamp or quoted ISO date string, and Mongoose Studio updates the filter in place.
